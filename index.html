OUR SERVICES

Swimming Pool

Set in the central courtyard of the hotel, with a natural well on a side, the swimming pool beckons for a lazy swim, a refreshing lap or just a nice snooze under the sun.

Ayurmana

At Ayurmana, we believe in the profound wisdom of ancient healing traditions, seamlessly blending with modern luxury to create a sanctuary for your well-being.

Library & Coffee Lounge

Enjoy a leisurely read of one of our books, even feel free to add to our collection, at the Library that looks down unto the courtyard while you enjoy a snack or a local coffee at the coffee lounge.

Personal Bar

Enjoy the well-stocked bar with a wide assortment of choices in each room.

JETTY - SPECIALTY RESTAURANT

Offering choice delicacies from across the world on a platter, we invite you to anchor at the Jetty and relish varied cuisines that reflect the various cultures that once influenced Fort Kochi including authentic local Kerala flavours, pan Indian, pan European, British favorites, a special range of seafood choices and so much more.
